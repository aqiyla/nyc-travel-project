Why New York City?

NYC - the city of dreams

New York, often called New York City or simply NYC, is the most populous city in the United States, located at the southern tip of New York State on one of the world's largest natural harbors. The city comprises five boroughs, each of which is coextensive with a respective county.

It is a global city and a cultural, financial, high-tech, entertainment, and media center with a significant influence on commerce, health care, scientific output, life sciences, research, technology, education, politics, tourism, dining, art, fashion, and sports. Home to the headquarters of the United Nations, New York is an important center for international diplomacy, and it is sometimes described as the world's most important city and the capital of the world.

My favorite eats in NYC

Sugarcane

Sugarcane is a stylish Caribbean restaurant located in the heart of Brooklyn. The menu goes beyond typical island exotic food and incorporates a bit of East Indian, Chinese and African flavors as well. Add in the fun atmosphere complete with uplifting music and it is easy to see why it was voted best Caribbean restaurant in New York.

Address:

238 Flatbush Ave, Brooklyn, NY 11217

What I like about it

Great food, drinks, music and ambiance.

Tatiana by Chef Kwame

Tatiana by Kwame Onwuachi steps right into this moment. It gives us food from cultures that are part of Mr. Onwuachi’s world but that weren’t usually on display at Lincoln Center, to put it mildly. Afro Caribbean-inspired dining with NYC influences.

Address:

10 Lincoln Center Plaza, New York, NY 10023

What I like about it

Moody and calm vibes with excellent food and service.

Little Ruby's

Little Ruby’s has brought Australian cafe culture to New York City, offering casual, locally sourced cuisine, specialty drinks, and a full coffee program. Ruby’s iconic menu features our signature bronte burger, taste-driven pasta, bowls and salads, and brunch classics like ricotta hotcakes.

Address:

225 West 4th Street, New York, NY 10014

What I like about it

All day cafe that serves great coffee and awesome truffle fries.
